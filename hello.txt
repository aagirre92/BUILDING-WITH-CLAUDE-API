A Gentle Hello

Hello to the morning sun so bright,
That paints the world with golden light.
Hello to the birds that sing their song,
And help us feel like we belong.

Hello to the flowers in full bloom,
That fill the air with sweet perfume.
Hello to the friends both near and far,
Who remind us just how loved we are.

Hello to each new day we greet,
With open hearts and dancing feet.
For every hello brings something new,
A chance to start the world anew.

So here's a hello from me to you,
May joy and wonder see you through!
